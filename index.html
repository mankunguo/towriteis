To Write is...

‘To Write Is...’ looks beyond text as the only manifestation
of writing, instead explores the hidden space between thought and words. The six writing tools are intentionally designed to
amplify specific motions inherent in the physical and mental act
of writing, whether it be hesitating, capturing, arranging, or reiterating. By making aware of the invisible gestures and subconscious processes, the project aims to reframe our role in the writing process and empower the user to explore, disrupt,
and reshape language.

@Mankun Guo
